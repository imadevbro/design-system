Brand Guidelines

Color

We selected this color palette for Elevate Consulting to convey a sense of stability, trustworthiness, and sophistication. The combination of teal blue and teal green provides a calming and reassuring effect, while the black adds a touch of professionalism and elegance. The white creates a sense of openness and clarity, making it easier for clients to engage with our services. Overall, this color palette communicates our values and mission as a consulting firm, helping us to establish a strong and memorable brand identity.
